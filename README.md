# Inventory Optimization & Sales Analytics

## Overview
Developed an end-to-end inventory analytics solution using SQL, Python, and Power BI to identify inventory inefficiencies, optimize stock management, and generate actionable business insights. The project combines inventory analysis, sales analytics, ABC-FSN classification, demand forecasting evaluation, and pricing analysis.

## Objectives
- Analyze inventory and sales performance
- Identify low-stock and overstock situations
- Evaluate demand forecast accuracy
- Perform ABC and FSN inventory classification
- Optimize reorder point decisions
- Assess pricing competitiveness
- Generate business recommendations

## Key Analyses Performed

### Revenue & Sales Analysis
- Category-wise revenue analysis
- Region-wise sales performance
- Store-level performance evaluation
- Top-selling product identification

### Inventory Analysis
- Inventory movement ratio calculation
- Reorder point analysis
- Low-stock detection
- Stock replenishment recommendations

### Demand Forecast Analysis
- Forecast error evaluation
- Demand gap identification
- Inventory vs forecast comparison

### ABC Analysis
Products classified based on cumulative revenue contribution:
- A: High-value products
- B: Moderate-value products
- C: Low-value products

### FSN Analysis
Products classified based on inventory movement:
- F: Fast Moving
- S: Slow Moving
- N: Non Moving

### Pricing Analysis
- Competitor price comparison
- Price difference analysis
- Impact of pricing on sales

## Dashboard Features
- Revenue overview
- Inventory health metrics
- ABC-FSN distribution
- Reorder point monitoring
- Sales trends
- Product performance insights

## Key Insights
- A small percentage of products contribute the majority of revenue.
- Fast-moving products require close inventory monitoring.
- Certain products are consistently under-forecasted.
- Pricing differences influence sales performance.
- Reorder point monitoring can reduce stockout risk.

## Business Recommendations
- Prioritize inventory control for A-category products.
- Reduce investment in non-moving inventory.
- Improve demand forecasting accuracy.
- Automate reorder point monitoring.
- Optimize pricing using competitor benchmarks.
